Know more about Air Quality Index (AQI)

1. The air quality index (AQI) is an index for reporting air quality on a daily basis.
2. It's a measure of how air pollution affects one's health within a short period of time.
3. There is increase in pollution due to vehicle traffic, forest fires, or anything that can increase air pollution. Pollutants tested include ozone, nitrogen dioxide, sulphur dioxide, among others.
